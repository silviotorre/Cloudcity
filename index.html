Time ago, in a cloud far away...

Innovation cost a lot of money, much more in a lack of standards. Interoperability is also a crucial factor, and security too.

In late spring 2013 Silvio Torre and Claudio Quaranta did a one-off conference about this in Orvieto, Italy, talking about open technology, design ed the necessary vision to step forward the smartcity paradigm.

"We hope in few year to see great number of smart cities coming up with their services and urban network ecosystems, but nobody want a massive number of apps and different account to do this for all these cities." (S. Torre on Datamanager magazine)

Maybe too early but Cloudcity was born.
Now is time to build a federative system, in a solid framework, accessible and lightning fast as the user needs.

Cloudcity, a smart cities interoperability framework

The Framework

Cloudcity is the response needed to interoperability and front end accessibility and convergence for our smart-cities constellation.

Designed in a solid Java based micro-services architecture, developed in a Model View Controller (MVC) pattern, with a state of the art front-end "ready to customize" code.

Cloudcity bring a new paradigm in governative and industrial vision, a "made in Europe" very clear roadmap forward, and towards NextGenerationEU iniziative.
